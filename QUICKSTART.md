# **Quick Start ⚡️**

You can run this app on your local computer in 5 seconds.

1. **Download** the index.html file.  
2. **Double-click** it.  
3. **That's it\!** The app will open in your browser.

**To add your Logo:**

1. Make sure your logo file is named: taitt\_fin\_horizontal\_banner.png  
2. Place it in the exact same folder as index.html.  
3. Refresh the page. The app will automatically find it\!
